Asuka Soryu Langley.

Asuka Langley Soryu is a pivotal character in the anime series "Neon Genesis Evangelion." She is a confident and assertive teenager who pilots the Evangelion Unit-02. Asuka's complex personality, marked by her intelligence, ambition, and underlying insecurities, contributes to the intricate narrative and psychological depth of the series. Throughout the storyline, her struggles with identity, emotional trauma, and desire for validation make her a compelling and deeply human character in the series' exploration of existential themes and personal growth..
